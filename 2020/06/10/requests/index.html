python(requests)

python之requests模块学习

  1. 导入模块
    import requests

  2. 选取需要的请求方式

  • GET
    无参数：r=requests.get(‘url’) 
    r=requests.get('https://www.baidu.com')
    有参数: r=requests.get(‘url’,params={‘参数名1’:’参数值1’,’参数名2’:’参数值2’}….) 
    r=requests.get('http://127.0.0.1/sqli/sqli-labs-master/Less-2/',params={'id':'1'})

  • POST
    payload = {‘key1’: ‘value1’, ‘key2’: ‘value2’}
    r=requests.post(‘url’,data=payload)

  • PUT,DELETE,HEAD,OPTIONS
    requests.put(‘url’)
    requests.delete(‘url’)
    requests.head(‘url’)
    requests.options(‘url’)
